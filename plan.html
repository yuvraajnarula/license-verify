Our Plan

Our startup will operate on a B2B business model. We will work with traffic police and state government to promote and support our app. Our main source of revenue will be individual contracts with Car Manufacturers that will allow them to use our app in their cars and make fingerprint verification mandatory. Our app shall also have advertisements for additional revenue. However, under no circumstances shall our app be paid for consumers

Target Audience

Parents

- Most parents (according to a survey by TOI) do not want their kids driving a car before age 18
- Can not keep an eye on their child all the time
- Our app makes sure they do not drive a car

Car Owners in an unsafe locality

- Have recently bought a car but are
worried it will be stolen
- Our app's theft protection ensures no unauthorized person is driving their car

Traffic Police

- Often unsure who was driving at the time of an accident
- Can use our app to check who was last logged in
- Increases accountability in hit and run cases
