How it Works?

The user logs into the app and registers his vehicle with the given UID and model number. He can add users who are authorized to drive the vehicle. These users are then verified with the government database.

When the vehicle is turned on, the user is asked to verify his fingerprint and only if his fingerprint is stored in the app, the vehicle will be turned on

The vehicle is configured (by the manufacturer) to not turn on without a verification signal from our app. If someone tries to do this, the theft alarm goes off

App's Feature

Anti-Theft Protection


Our app also protects against car theft. Since a verified driver is required to start the car, no unknown person can start the car. Incase , someone tries to the anti-theft alarm in the car will go off

Accident Verification


Police are often unable to verify who was driving in case of road accidents. However, using our app, they will be able to find out who last accessed the car and they will be liable to the requiste punishment. This will increase accountability in hit and run cases
